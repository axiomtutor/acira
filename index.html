Unit 1: Real Number Axioms

0002 Groups

Let $X$ be a nonmepty set and $\ast:X^2\to X$ an operation on $X$. If $e\in X$ is such that $$ \forall x \in X, \quad x\ast e = e\ast x = x $$ then we say that $e$ is an identity for $\ast$.
Theorem: Every bounded sequence has a convergent subsequence.
