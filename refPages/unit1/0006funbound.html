0006 Function Bounds

Let $A,B,C$ be nonempty sets, $f,g:A\to B$ and $h:A\times B\to C$. We define the image of $f$ as $$ \text{Im}(f) = \{f(x) : x\in X\} $$ For any $x\in A$ we define the $h$ with $x$ fixed to be the function $h_{(x,\cdot)}:B\to C$ defined by $$ h_{(x,\cdot)}(y) = h(x,y), \quad \forall y\in B $$
